💧 WaterLog — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

WaterLog is a simple daily water intake tracking app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains clearly what data we collect (very little) and how it is used.

Data We Collect

WaterLog stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your daily water intake log (amounts and dates)
  • Your daily hydration goal
  • Your preferred unit of measurement (ml or oz)
  • Your notification reminder preferences

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, database, or third party. It is not visible to us or anyone else.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not require account registration of any kind
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not use advertising networks that collect personal data

Notifications

If you enable the daily reminder, WaterLog will request permission to send you notifications. This permission is entirely optional and can be revoked at any time in your device settings. Notification preferences are stored locally on your device only.

Purchases

WaterLog offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to remove advertisements. This purchase is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store any payment information. Please refer to Google Play's privacy policy for information on how purchase data is handled.

Advertisements

The free version of WaterLog displays advertisements. These ads are served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device information to serve relevant ads in accordance with Google's privacy policy. You can remove all ads with the one-time in-app purchase.

Children's Privacy

WaterLog is a general wellness app suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all WaterLog data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings, or by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the WaterLog app, please contact us at the email address listed on our Google Play developer page.
